## Title

Bayesian Analysis of Kleiber's Law and Metabolic Scaling

## Project URL

https://github.com/Amir-SaiediSaber/kleiber-law-bayesian-analysis

## Description

Developed a Bayesian analysis in R to test whether basal metabolic rate follows Kleiber's three-quarter power law across 279 bird and mammal observations. Implemented a conjugate Gibbs sampler from scratch and compared fixed, free-slope, and group-dependent regression models using convergence diagnostics, DIC, posterior predictive checks, and independent JAGS validation. The pooled exponent was 0.536 (95% credible interval: 0.504-0.568), and a group-dependent model revealed distinct scaling relationships for birds and mammals.
